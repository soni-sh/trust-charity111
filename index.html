We are dedicated to serving humanity
since our foundation.

Akhanda Bharat Satyamev Jayate Jagatkalyaan Mandir Seva Trust was founded with a deep commitment to the welfare of society. Our primary mission is to promote peace, prosperity, and education among underprivileged communities. We believe that through service and spiritual guidance, we can help create a more just and compassionate world. Our focus includes healthcare initiatives, community development, and spreading positive spiritual teachings.
  • Providing healthcare to underserved communities
  • Supporting education and skill development programs
  • Fostering spiritual growth and harmony

Welcome to the Jagatkalyan Mandir Seva Trust, an organization dedicated to serving humanity with compassion, faith, and unity. The trust is committed to the spiritual, cultural, and social upliftment of society, promoting the values of unity, peace, and harmony. Through its various services and initiatives, it aims to bring about positive change and ensure the well-being of all. Guided by the core belief of "सत्यमेव जयते," we strive to create a world where truth, kindness, and service to humanity prevail.

Founded by गिता देवी, the trust continues her legacy of selfless service, spreading love and devotion to all. Join us on this journey of spiritual and social transformation as we work towards a brighter future for all.
